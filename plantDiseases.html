Olive Diseases

Olive Fruit Fly

Type: Insect

Symptoms: Fruit drop before ripening, internal rotting, small tunnels inside fruit.

Impact: Reduces olive oil production, lowers oil quality, affects exports and food security.

Solutions: Use pheromone traps, safe insecticides, remove and destroy infected fruits.

Olive Knot

Type: Bacterial Disease

Impact: Weakens the tree, reduces sap flow, lowers fruit production.

Solutions: Prune infected branches, disinfect tools, apply copper-based sprays.

Symptoms: Rough, knot-like growths on branches and trunk, often at pruning wounds.

Peacock Spot

Type: Fungal Disease

Symptoms: Circular dark spots on leaves, leaf drop, reduced tree vitality.

Impact: Lowers photosynthesis, weakens the tree, reduces overall yield.

Solutions: Improve air circulation, apply copper-based fungicides, remove fallen leaves.

Tomato & Cucumber Diseases

Powdery Mildew

Type: Fungus

Symptoms: White powdery spots on leaves, yellowing, stunted growth.

Impact: Reduces yield, weakens plant growth, affects market supply.

Solutions: Apply fungicides, plant resistant varieties, ensure proper ventilation.

Early Blight

Type: Fungus

Symptoms: Dark circular spots on leaves and stems, wilting, premature leaf drop.

Impact: Causes severe crop losses, reduces tomato and cucumber supply.

Solutions: Crop rotation, fungicide sprays, proper plant spacing.

Bacterial Spot

Type: Bacterial Disease

Symptoms: Small, dark, water-soaked spots on leaves, stems, and fruit.

Impact: causes leaf loss, reduces yield, and increases vulnerability to other diseases.

Solutions: Use certified disease-free seeds, apply copper-based sprays, practice crop rotation, avoid working with wet plants.
